The 3 Secrets of David

2nd King of Israel – A Man after God's Own Heart

What made David, a flawed and often broken man, someone God called “a man after My own heart”? This book explores David’s life as a shepherd, poet, warrior, king, and relentless God-pursuer.

Through scripture-rich reflection and practical application, the book reveals three timeless principles that shaped David’s walk with God and continue to speak to believers today.

🙏 Worship that moves heaven
🛡 Trust that withstands giants
👑 Obedience that leaves a legacy
📖 Scripture, prayer & reflection
